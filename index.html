About SweetBite Bakery

Welcome to SweetBite Bakery, a family-run bakery built on tradition, love, and the joy of sharing sweet moments. We’ve been baking fresh, homemade cakes, cookies, pastries, and breads for generations using time-tested family recipes. Our bakery isn’t just a place to grab a treat—it’s a cozy spot to relax with a cup of tea or coffee and enjoy the little things. Whether you’re celebrating something special or just in the mood for something sweet, we’re here to make your day better. We use only high-quality ingredients and put care into every batch we bake. Now, we’re excited to welcome you both in-store and online. Browse, order, or stop by—either way, you’re always welcome. SweetBite Bakery – come in as a guest, leave as family.
